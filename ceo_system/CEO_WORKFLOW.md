# Concrete Conversations CEO Workflow

## Weekly CEO Goal
Book speaking opportunities, grow brand awareness, and publish consistent content.

## Daily Business Rhythm

### Monday - Strategy
- Pick weekly theme
- Create podcast topic
- Create YouTube topic
- Build outreach list

### Tuesday - Content Creation
- Write podcast outline
- Write YouTube script
- Create 10 social captions

### Wednesday - Outreach
- Draft 10 emails to schools, youth programs, churches, nonprofits, and sports programs

### Thursday - Follow-Up
- Draft follow-up emails
- Track responses
- Update lead list

### Friday - Publishing
- Finalize YouTube description
- Finalize podcast title
- Prepare weekend posts

## CEO Metrics
- Outreach emails drafted
- Responses received
- Meetings booked
- Videos planned
- Posts created
- Speaking opportunities opened
