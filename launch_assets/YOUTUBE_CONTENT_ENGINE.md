# Concrete Motivation YouTube Content Engine

Purpose: make YouTube a repeatable growth channel for Concrete Motivation and Concrete Conversations.

## Weekly YouTube Rhythm

### Anchor Video
One 8-15 minute video every week.

Structure:
1. Hook: name the pressure in the first 5 seconds.
2. Promise: tell the viewer what they will be able to do by the end.
3. Story: make it real.
4. Lesson: explain the principle.
5. Action: give a concrete step.
6. Challenge: ask for a commitment in the comments.

### Shorts
Create 5 Shorts from every anchor video:

1. Hook Short
2. Story Short
3. Lesson Short
4. Faith/Family/Leadership Short
5. Challenge Short
