# Concrete Motivation 90-Day Content and Outreach Campaign

## 90-Day Mission
Build audience, proof, leads, speaking opportunities, and revenue.

## Main Targets
- 90 short-form posts
- 12 podcast episodes or episode outlines
- 300 outreach messages
- 100 qualified leads
- 20 discovery calls
- 5 speaking engagements
- 3 sponsor conversations

## Weekly Content Minimum
- 5 short videos
- 3 Facebook posts
- 3 LinkedIn posts
- 1 podcast episode or trailer
- 1 email/newsletter
- 20 outreach messages

## Month 1: Foundation and Proof
Theme: Built Under Pressure

Goals:
- Launch social pages.
- Publish first 30 posts.
- Record first podcast trailer and Episode 1.
- Build first 50 leads.
- Secure 1-2 pilot talks.

Weekly outreach:
- 10 schools
- 5 youth programs
- 3 churches
- 2 podcast guests

## Month 2: Authority and Partnerships
Theme: Discipline Under Pressure

Goals:
- Publish 30 more posts.
- Create a speaking one-sheet.
- Get testimonials from pilot talks.
- Begin sponsor conversations.
- Book 2-3 paid opportunities.

Weekly outreach:
- 10 schools
- 5 sports teams
- 3 churches
- 2 sponsors

## Month 3: Revenue and Scale
Theme: Leadership That Holds

Goals:
- Publish 30 more posts.
- Turn best videos into website proof.
- Start email list.
- Build a repeatable booking process.
- Close paid speaking dates.

Weekly outreach:
- 10 schools or organizations
- 5 companies or sponsors
- 3 podcast guests
- 2 follow-up blocks

## Daily Execution
Morning:
- Post 1 piece of content.
- Send 5 outreach messages.

Afternoon:
- Engage with comments.
- Add leads to CRM.

Evening:
- Record or outline the next piece of content.
- Review responses.

## Sunday Review
- What content got the most response?
- Which outreach message created replies?
- Which audience showed the most demand?
- What proof can be added to the website?
- What needs to be repeated next week?
