# Concrete Motivation School Outreach Engine

Purpose: book school, athletic, youth, and community speaking opportunities.

1. High school principals
2. Assistant principals
3. Athletic directors
4. Football and basketball coaches
5. School counselors
6. Student support teams
7. College athletic departments
8. Youth development nonprofits
9. Church youth ministries
10. Community centers

## Core Speaking Offers

### Offer 1: Pressure Has a Purpose
Audience: students, athletes, young adults
Outcome: students identify pressure, convert it into a disciplined next step, and commit to a seven-day action challenge.

### Offer 2: Built Under Pressure
Audience: athletes and teams
Outcome: athletes connect discipline, accountability, identity, and leadership beyond the scoreboard.

### Offer 3: One Brick at a Time
Audience: students rebuilding from setbacks
Outcome: students learn how small daily promises create trust, confidence, and direction.

### Offer 4: The Discipline Gap
Audience: school leaders, student groups, athletes
Outcome: participants learn why motivation fades and how structure keeps them moving.

## Call Script

1. Thank them for taking the call.
2. Ask what students are facing right now.
3. Listen for pressure points: attendance, behavior, motivation, grief, discipline, athletics, confidence.
4. Match one Concrete Motivation topic to their need.
5. Offer a simple session outline.
6. Ask for the next step: date, decision maker, budget, or pilot session.
