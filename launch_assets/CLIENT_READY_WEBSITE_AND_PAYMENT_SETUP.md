# Concrete Motivation Client-Ready Website and Payment Setup

## Website Goal
Turn the website into a client intake and booking machine for speaking engagements, podcast guests, youth programs, schools, churches, teams, and sponsors.

## Production Pages
1. Home
2. About
3. Speaking
4. Podcast
5. Programs
6. Book Jaytee
7. Contact
8. Payment / Deposit

## Required Integrations

### Booking Calendar
Recommended: Calendly

Create event types:
- 15-minute intro call
- 30-minute speaking discovery call
- 45-minute podcast guest prep call
- 60-minute partner strategy call

Add the Calendly booking link to every Book Jaytee button.

### Payment
Recommended: Stripe Payment Links first, then full checkout later.

Create payment links:
- Community / Pilot Talk Deposit: $100
- Student Leadership Talk Deposit: $250
- Team / Youth Workshop Deposit: $500
- Keynote Deposit: $750
- Corporate Leadership Session Deposit: $1,000

Website CTA wording:
- Book a Discovery Call
- Reserve a Date
- Pay Deposit
- Sponsor an Episode

### Contact Form
Recommended: Tally, Typeform, Google Forms, or Formspree.

Required fields:
- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Organization
- Event type
- Audience size
- Preferred date
- Budget range
- Location / virtual
- Message

### Email Capture
Recommended: ConvertKit, Mailchimp, Beehiiv, or Google Form starter.

Lead magnet:
The Concrete Challenge: 7 Days to Build Discipline Under Pressure

## Speaking Packages

### Community Starter
Best for youth groups, community centers, and small rooms.
- 30-minute message
- Q&A
- Photo / testimonial request
Starting at: Free to $500 during proof-building phase

### Student Leadership Talk
Best for schools and student groups.
- 45-minute keynote
- Discipline and accountability framework
- Student challenge
Starting at: $500-$1,500

### Team Culture Session
Best for athletes, coaches, and organizations.
- 60-minute session
- Team identity and accountability focus
- Practical leadership challenge
Starting at: $1,000-$2,500

### Corporate Leadership Session
Best for operators, managers, and frontline teams.
- 60-90 minute keynote or workshop
- Leadership under pressure
- Team ownership and execution
Starting at: $2,500+

## Website Trust Builders
Add as soon as available:
- Video clips
- Event photos
- Testimonials
- School / team logos with permission
- Podcast clips
- Speaking one-sheet PDF
- Press kit

## Final Website QA
- All buttons go somewhere real.
- No placeholder links remain.
- Mobile version looks clean.
- Contact form sends confirmation.
- Payments are test-purchased with a small amount first.
- Calendar confirmation email works.
- Website copy has no typos.
- Every page has one clear call to action.
