# Concrete Motivation Sponsorship Engine

Purpose: build sponsor revenue for Concrete Motivation, Concrete Conversations, YouTube, live events, and community impact work.

## Sponsor Categories

1. Local gyms and trainers
2. Barbershops
3. Car dealerships
4. Credit unions and banks
5. Insurance agencies
6. Restaurants and cafes
7. Athletic brands
8. Youth nonprofits
9. Real estate professionals
10. Mental health and wellness providers

## Sponsor Offer Ladder

### Community Supporter - $250
- Mention in one podcast episode
- Thank-you social post
- Logo/name in community recap

### Episode Sponsor - $500
- Sponsor mention in one Concrete Conversations episode
- One short-form clip caption mention
- Website/social thank-you

### Monthly Partner - $1,500
- Four podcast mentions
- Four social mentions
- Logo/name on monthly partner page
- One community impact feature

### Event Partner - $2,500+
- Live event mention
- Table or flyer placement where appropriate
- Logo/name on event recap
- Podcast and social coverage

## Sponsor Email

I believe [Business Name] could be a strong community-aligned partner because you already serve people who care about growth, trust, and real-life impact.

I would like to explore a simple sponsorship or community partnership that gives your brand visibility while helping us create more motivational content, student sessions, and local impact.

Would you be open to a brief conversation next week?

Respectfully,
Jaytee Miller
Concrete Motivation

## Sponsor Call Questions

1. What audience does your business most want to reach?
2. What community causes matter to you?
3. Are you more interested in podcast visibility, event visibility, or youth impact?
4. What monthly sponsorship range is comfortable for testing?
5. What would make this partnership feel successful after 90 days?

## First Sponsor Sprint

- Build 50 sponsor leads.
- Send 10 per day for 5 days.
- Follow up after 2 business days.
- Offer a low-risk pilot.
- Request one intro from every no-fit reply.
