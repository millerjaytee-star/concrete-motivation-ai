# Concrete Conversations Podcast Guest Engine

Purpose: keep Concrete Conversations booked with guests who strengthen the Concrete Motivation brand.

## Guest Categories

1. Athletes with comeback stories
2. Coaches and trainers
3. Principals and educators
4. Fathers and family leaders
5. Entrepreneurs who built from adversity
6. Faith leaders
7. Community organizers
8. Mental health professionals
9. Local business owners who support youth
10. Community leaders with stories of resilience

## Guest Fit Score

Score each potential guest from 1 to 5:

| Factor | Score |
|---|---:|
| Real adversity story | 1-5 |
| Practical lessons | 1-5 |
| Audience overlap | 1-5 |
| Willing to promote episode | 1-5 |
| Strong short-form clip potential | 1-5 |

Priority guests score 18+.

## Standard Episode Structure

1. Cold open clip moment
2. Welcome and guest introduction
3. Life before the pressure
4. The defining challenge
5. The turning point
6. The discipline that changed things
7. Faith/family/leadership lesson
8. Advice to someone in the middle of it
9. Concrete Challenge
10. Closing and guest links

## Guest Prep Questions

1. What pressure shaped you the most?
2. What did people not see while you were going through it?
3. What habit or decision helped you rebuild?
4. Who held you accountable?
5. What do you believe now that you did not understand before?
6. What would you tell a young person going through pressure right now?
7. What is one concrete action listeners should take this week?
