## Video 1: Built Under Pressure

### Title
Built Under Pressure

### Hook
You are not weak. You are under construction.

### Script
Some seasons feel heavy because they are building something in you. Concrete does not become strong without pressure, and neither do people who are called to lead. Stop looking at pressure like punishment. Start asking what this season is trying to build in you. Today, do one thing with discipline, even if your emotions are not ready. That is how the foundation gets stronger.

### Caption
Built under pressure. Proven through purpose. Do one disciplined thing today that your future self will thank you for.

## Video 2: Discipline After Motivation Leaves

### Title
Discipline After Motivation Leaves

### Hook
Motivation starts the fire. Discipline keeps the lights on.

### Script
Everybody loves motivation when it feels good. But the real test comes when the feeling leaves, the pressure gets loud, and nobody is clapping for you. That is when discipline has to take over. Discipline is keeping the promise after the emotion disappears. Pick one promise today. Keep it. Then do it again tomorrow.

### Caption
Discipline is what motivation looks like when the feeling is gone.

## Video 3: The Leadership Standard

### Title
The Leadership Standard

### Hook
The people depending on you need your consistency more than your excuses.

### Script
Leadership is not a title. Leadership is what people experience when life gets heavy. Your family, your team, your business, and your community do not need a perfect version of you. They need a present, accountable, disciplined version of you. If you want to lead, start by keeping your word in the small things. That is where the standard is built.

### Caption
Leadership starts with the promises nobody sees you keep.
