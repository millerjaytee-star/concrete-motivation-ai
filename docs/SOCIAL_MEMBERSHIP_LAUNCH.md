# Social Membership Launch

This generator creates a 14-day launch campaign for Concrete Builders Membership.

## Outputs
- 14 Instagram captions
- 14 Facebook posts
- 14 LinkedIn posts
- 14 short Reel scripts
- 14 YouTube Community posts
- 7 newsletter angles
- Daily CTA calendar

## Run it
```bash
python3 scripts/create_social_sales_campaign.py --offer "Concrete Builders Membership" --days 14
```

## Safety
- Draft only.
- Do not auto-post.
- Keep the CTA tied to membership or booking.
