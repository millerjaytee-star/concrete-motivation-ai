# YouTube Launch Kit

Version 7 adds a YouTube launch kit in `youtube_launch/` for Concrete Motivation and Concrete Conversations.

## Files

- `CHANNEL_SETUP.md`: channel name options, setup checklist, playlists, launch rhythm, and website/output vault connection.
- `CHANNEL_COPY.md`: paste-ready channel description, bios, about copy, links copy, pinned comments, Community posts, and booking inquiry blurb.
- `VIDEO_TEMPLATES.md`: reusable long-form, Concrete Conversations, and Shorts video structures.
- `FIRST_10_VIDEOS.md`: titles, descriptions, outlines, hooks, and CTAs for the first 10 videos.
- `SHORTS_SYSTEM.md`: 15-second, 30-second, and 60-second Shorts formats with hook, CTA, caption, and repurposing banks.
- `THUMBNAIL_GUIDE.md`: black/concrete/gold thumbnail direction and first 10 thumbnail text examples.
- `UPLOAD_CHECKLIST.md`: repeatable upload and post-publish checklist.

## Workflow

Use the website as the public home and booking destination. Use the output vault to save scripts, captions, pinned comments, thumbnail text, upload notes, and repurposed Shorts plans. No YouTube API, posting automation, payments, or deployment are included in Version 7.
