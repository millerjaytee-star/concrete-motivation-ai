- Gentle warmth
- Presence enhancement
- Compression
- De-essing when needed
- Limiting
- Loudness normalization

Target:

- Approximately -14 to -16 LUFS integrated
- True peak below -1 dBTP
- Clean narration on phone speakers
- No distortion
- No clipped peaks
- No silent narration gaps

Use original, properly licensed or generated background music only.

Do not use copyrighted commercial music without permission.
