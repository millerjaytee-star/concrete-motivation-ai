# YouTube Channel Setup

## Channel Name Options

1. Concrete Motivation
2. Concrete Conversations
3. Concrete Motivation with Jaytee Miller

Recommended launch name: **Concrete Motivation with Jaytee Miller**.

This keeps the main brand visible while making the channel personal, searchable, and clear for speaking inquiries.

## Channel Purpose

Concrete Motivation is a motivational speaking and podcast channel built for athletes, fathers, young men, leaders, and people rebuilding under pressure. The channel turns real-life pressure into disciplined action through original messages, Concrete Conversations clips, leadership lessons, youth and athlete development, family-centered legacy content, and practical comeback frameworks.

## Brand Positioning

- Direct, disciplined, and real
- Leadership-driven and family-centered
- Faith-aware but not forced
- Built for people carrying pressure and still choosing execution
- Practical enough for schools, teams, churches, businesses, and athletes

## Setup Checklist

- Create or claim the YouTube channel.
- Set channel name to the selected launch name.
- Add channel handle when available, such as `@ConcreteMotivation`.
- Upload profile image or logo.
- Upload banner art using black, concrete gray, white, and gold accents.
- Add channel description from `CHANNEL_COPY.md`.
- Add website link once the static site is published.
- Add social links when active.
- Create playlists:
  - Concrete Conversations
  - Motivation and Mindset
  - Fatherhood and Legacy
  - Athlete Leadership
  - Shorts
- Add the first trailer or featured video.
- Pin the strongest launch video to the channel homepage.

## Launch Playlists

### Motivation and Mindset

Original messages about pressure, discipline, purpose, setbacks, and execution.

### Concrete Conversations

Podcast episodes, guest conversations, live talks, and long-form clips.

### Fatherhood and Legacy

Messages for fathers, young men, family leaders, and people building something bigger than themselves.

### Athlete Leadership

Mindset and leadership content for athletes, coaches, teams, students, and youth programs.

### Shorts

15, 30, and 60 second clips pulled from speeches, podcast moments, and direct-to-camera lessons.

## Launch Rhythm

- Publish 1 long-form video or podcast episode each week.
- Publish 3 to 5 Shorts each week.
- Post 1 Community post before each long-form release.
- Post 1 Community post after each release asking for comments or questions.
- Add every strong quote, story, or lesson to the output vault for reuse.

## Connection to Website and Output Vault

The website should point visitors to the channel through the Concrete Conversations section and booking calls to action. The output vault should store scripts, episode outlines, captions, pinned comments, thumbnail text, and upload notes so each video can become a reusable brand asset.
