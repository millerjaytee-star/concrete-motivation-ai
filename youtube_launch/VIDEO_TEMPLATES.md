# Video Templates

## Long-Form Motivation Template

### Structure

1. Cold open: one direct truth in the first 5 seconds.
2. Name the pressure: describe the real struggle without exaggeration.
3. Concrete Motivation principle: connect pressure to discipline, purpose, leadership, family, or faith-aware action.
4. Story or example: keep it grounded and original.
5. Practical framework: 3 steps the viewer can use today.
6. Challenge: one measurable action.
7. Call to action: subscribe, comment the promise, share with someone rebuilding, and visit the website for booking.

## Concrete Conversations Template

### Structure

1. Cold open clip: strongest quote or tension.
2. Intro: who the guest is or what the conversation is about.
3. Pressure point: what made the topic real.
4. Lesson: what the story taught.
5. Practical action: what the listener can do.
6. Reflection question: prompt comments.
7. Booking or subscribe CTA.

### Description Template

## Direct-to-Camera Short Template

### 15 Seconds

- 0-3s: hard truth hook
- 3-10s: one clear lesson
- 10-15s: one action or comment CTA

### 30 Seconds

- 0-4s: hook
- 4-12s: pressure or story setup
- 12-24s: Concrete Motivation principle
- 24-30s: one action

### 60 Seconds

- 0-5s: hook
- 5-15s: name the problem
- 15-35s: unpack the lesson
- 35-50s: give the action
- 50-60s: CTA and challenge
