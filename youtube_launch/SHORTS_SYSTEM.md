# YouTube Shorts System

## Purpose

Shorts should create daily entry points into Concrete Motivation and Concrete Conversations. Each Short should deliver one truth, one practical action, and one reason to keep building.

## 15-Second Format

- 0-2s: hard hook
- 2-10s: one direct lesson
- 10-15s: action or comment CTA

Example:

Hook: "Pressure is not permission to quit."
Lesson: "It is information. It shows where your discipline needs structure."
CTA: "Comment the promise you are keeping today."

## 30-Second Format

- 0-3s: hook
- 3-10s: name the real pressure
- 10-23s: Concrete Motivation principle
- 23-30s: next action

## 60-Second Format

- 0-5s: hook
- 5-15s: pressure setup
- 15-35s: lesson or story
- 35-50s: practical action
- 50-60s: CTA and challenge

## Hook Bank

- Pressure is not permission to quit.
- Nobody claps for the work that builds you.
- Your family does not need perfect. They need present.
- Stop calling it failure before you collect the lesson.
- The scoreboard cannot tell you who you are becoming.
- You do not build legacy by waiting for life to get lighter.
- One brick today beats a perfect plan tomorrow.
- Pain does not become purpose until you build with it.
- Leadership is proven when life is heavy.
- Faith does not remove the work. It gives the work direction.

## CTA Bank

- Comment the one promise you will keep today.
- Send this to someone rebuilding.
- Save this for the day motivation gets quiet.
- Subscribe for Concrete Motivation and Concrete Conversations.
- Share this with a teammate, father, coach, or leader who needs it.
- Write your next action before you scroll.
- Come back in seven days and say what changed.

## Repurposing From Podcast Clips

1. Pull the strongest 45-60 second lesson.
2. Cut a 15-second version with the sharpest line.
3. Cut a 30-second version with the tension and lesson.
4. Cut a 60-second version with setup, story, and action.
5. Turn the transcript into:
   - A quote card
   - A carousel
   - A Community post question
   - A pinned comment
   - A future long-form outline
