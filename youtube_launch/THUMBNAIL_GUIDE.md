# Thumbnail Guide

## Style

- Black, concrete gray, white, and gold accents
- Concrete texture backgrounds
- Big bold words
- 2-4 word thumbnail text
- Strong contrast on mobile
- No fake luxury props
- No copied speaker styles
- No unsupported claims

## Expression and Emotion Direction

- Focused, locked-in, serious, or reflective
- Pressure without defeat
- Leadership without arrogance
- Calm intensity over theatrical hype
- For fatherhood or legacy topics: grounded and responsible
- For athlete topics: competitive, alert, and disciplined

## Background Ideas

- Concrete wall
- Empty gym or court lines
- Microphone and simple stage
- Training space
- Notebook or planning table
- Podcast desk
- Team hallway or locker-room-inspired texture

## Thumbnail Text Rules

- Use 2-4 words.
- Make the words emotional but honest.
- Avoid clickbait.
- Keep text readable at phone size.
- Use gold for one emphasis word only.

## Layout Formula

- Face or subject on one side
- 2-4 bold words on the other side
- Concrete texture background
- Small gold accent line or block
- No clutter
- Keep margins safe for YouTube cropping
