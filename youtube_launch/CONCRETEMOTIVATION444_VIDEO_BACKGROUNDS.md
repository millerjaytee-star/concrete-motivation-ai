## Overlay Style

- Use large uppercase white or warm stone text.
- Keep titles to 2-4 words when making thumbnails.
- Use gold for one accent word, line, or border.
- Keep the Concrete Motivation mark small and consistent.
- Avoid filling the whole image with text; let the background carry mood.
