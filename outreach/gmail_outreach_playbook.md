# Gmail Outreach Playbook

This system prepares outreach. It does not send email automatically.

## Approval Rule

Every message must be reviewed by Jaytee before sending. Gmail automation can draft, label, score, and queue contacts, but it must not send without explicit approval.

## Investor / Sponsor Draft

Subject: Concrete Motivation youth and leadership partnership

Hi [Name],

I am building Concrete Motivation to help athletes, fathers, students, teams, and leaders turn pressure into disciplined action.

I am reaching out because your work appears aligned with youth development, leadership, and community impact. I am preparing sponsor-supported Concrete Builder cohorts and would value a short conversation about whether this mission fits your giving, brand, or investment priorities.

Would you be open to a 15-minute conversation next week?

Jaytee Miller

## School / Team Draft

Subject: Practical leadership session for [School/Team]

Hi [Name],

I am Jaytee Miller with Concrete Motivation. I help athletes and young people build discipline, ownership, and leadership when pressure is real.

I would like to support [School/Team] with a direct team talk or youth leadership workshop that gives students one measurable action plan they can use immediately.

Would it make sense to send a one-page session outline?

Jaytee Miller

## Follow-Up Rule

Follow up once after 5-7 days if there is a clear fit signal. Do not keep pushing cold contacts who do not respond.
